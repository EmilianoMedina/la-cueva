Our Story

What started off as a small family business and became a South Bay Icon. La Cueva, is as a small, hole in the wall taqueria one block away from West Gate Church in 2010. Over the years, it has evolved in to a very popular go-to-spot in the Bay Area. From breakfast burritos, carne asada super, and regular tacos, our food is unique in every way. The food is incredibly tasty and is served fresh all the time. Also, the price is right and of course is the only place on the planet that serves the famous Green Sauce.

Yes, the Green Sauce is what we're known for. If you haven't had La Cueva, you need to get stop by and order anything on the menu! Don’t forget to ask for the Green Sauce!
